### **Operator Portal Design Notes:**

**Differences from Main App:**
- **Simplified navigation** (4 items vs 11)
- **No financial visibility** (no revenue, invoices, payments)
- **No other operators visible** (only see own schedule)
- **Read-only for most data** (cannot edit events, only availability)
- **No access to:**
  - CRM/Pipeline
  - Client management
  - Proposal builder
  - Contract management
  - Reports & analytics
  - System settings

**Visual Style:**
- Same tactical aesthetic (dark theme, cyan accents)
- Simplified top bar (no financial snapshot)
- Minimal sidebar (4 items)
- Larger touch targets (optimized for mobile use on-site)
